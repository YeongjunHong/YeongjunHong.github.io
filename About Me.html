--- layout: about title: About --- ## About Me

Hello World! I'm Yeongjun Hong, a recent graduate with a Master's degree in Artificial Intelligence. My passion lies in the intersection of AI and healthcare, with a particular focus on Medical AI. I'm deeply fascinated by the potential of AI to revolutionize clinical practices and improve patient outcomes. My research interests span several cutting-edge areas of AI, including:

  • Clinical AI applications
  • Knowledge Extraction techniques
  • Graph Networks and their applications in healthcare
  • eXplainable AI (XAI) for transparent and interpretable medical decision-making

I'm committed to advancing the field of Medical AI, working towards creating intelligent systems that can assist healthcare professionals in diagnosis, treatment planning, and patient care. Through my work, I aim to contribute to the development of more efficient, accurate, and explainable AI solutions in the medical domain.
